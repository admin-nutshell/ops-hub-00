## What the platform does today

A customer emails `support@inatechshell.ca`. Within minutes, without any human:

1. **FreeScout** fetches the email (polling every 60 seconds)
2. **Inngest** picks it up and sends it to **LiteLLM** (gpt-4o-mini)
3. AI classifies urgency (critical/high/normal/low), category, and routing
4. AI drafts a reply and posts it as an internal FreeScout note (visible to you)
5. Ticket state advances: `new → triaged → responded`
6. After 24 hours in `responded` state, ticket auto-resolves
7. On resolution, AI extracts a **KB article** from the conversation and saves it
8. **SLA monitor** checks every 5 minutes — if a ticket isn't responded to within 60 minutes, an alert note appears in FreeScout and an audit log entry is written
9. **LangFuse** records cost per ticket (target: < $1 USD at scale)

The app-agnostic design means onboarding a new tenant is a config change, not a code change.
